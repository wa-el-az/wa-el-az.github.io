# Contributing to Wael OS

First off, thanks for taking the time to contribute! 

## How to Contribute
1. **Fork the repo** and create your branch from `main`.
2. **Make your changes** (whether it's fixing CSS, adding a new Terminal command, or optimizing the Tea Clicker).
3. **Test your code** to ensure nothing breaks.
4. **Issue that pull request!**

## Reporting Bugs
If you find a bug, please use the Issue Tracker and provide as much detail as possible, including your browser and steps to reproduce the error.
